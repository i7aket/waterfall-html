Waterfall Interactive

Waterfall Interactive, founded on May 5, 2006, has released numerous successful projects over its history, which were showcased at international exhibitions and translated into many European languages. The games developed by Waterfall Interactive are styled as interactive fairy tales based on classic literature and include educational elements that aid in developing children's reading, arithmetic, and logical thinking skills. The company's casual projects are distinguished by their captivating plots and puzzles.

Services

We aim to offer a full range of services for realizing any gaming projects, combining innovative technologies and creative approach to create unique gaming experiences.

Game Development 

We craft unique gaming projects from concept to launch, including level design, programming, graphics, and sound design.

Game Design

We design engaging gameplay mechanics, game balance, and interfaces, creating unforgettable experiences for players.

2D and 3D Modeling and Animation

We develop two- and three-dimensional models of characters, worlds, and items, as well as animate their movements for implementation in games of various genres.

Game Animation

We create dynamic and expressive animations for characters, objects, and effects, bringing games to life with realism.

Music and Sound Design

We compose music tracks and create sound effects that emphasize the atmosphere and dynamics of the game.

Game Testing (QA)

We conduct comprehensive game testing for bugs and errors, analyze gameplay and balance, ensuring high product quality.

Game Support

We offer updates, bug fixes, and technical support post-launch, enhancing the gaming experience for users.

Mobile Development

We adapt games for mobile devices, optimizing control and graphics for the nuances of smartphones and tablets.

Development for Consoles and Online Platforms

We create games for gaming consoles and develop multiplayer online games, including services and game stores.

Monetization and Consulting

We develop game monetization strategies and provide consultancy services on marketing and trends in the gaming industry.

Additional Content Creation

We develop and implement additional levels, characters, campaigns, and downloadable content (DLC), expanding the gaming experience.

Blockchain Technology and Cryptocurrencies in Games

We integrate blockchain technology for creating cryptocurrency games, NFTs, and smart contracts, opening new possibilities for players and developers.
