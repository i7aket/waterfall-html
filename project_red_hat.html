"Little Red Riding Hood: Preschool Math" is an interactive adventure story for children aged four to seven years. High-quality imagery, three-dimensional locations, and realistic animation will captivate any audience. The plot is based on the tale of the famous French writer Charles Perrault "Little Red Riding Hood".
 
Once upon a time, there was a sweet little girl named Little Red Riding Hood. One day her mother sent her to deliver some pies to her grandmother. All would have been fine, but the path to grandmother's house goes through the woods, and the Wolf living in the woods smelled the tempting scent of the pies and clearly decided to treat himself at all costs. Devising a cunning plan, the Wolf rushes to the grandmother's house and, to delay Little Red Riding Hood, destroys the only bridge on the road. The little girl must use all her wits and ingenuity to defeat the evil wolf's schemes, reach her grandmother's house safe and sound, and save her grandmother from the teeth of the gray bandit.
 
Waterfall Interactive presents a game with modern, beautiful three-dimensional graphics that will help any player, regardless of age, enjoy the gameplay to the fullest.
The interactive world of the game, memorable characters, educational mini-games, and fun animated videos – all this will not only provide a pleasant gaming experience but also help young players gain valuable experience in solving logical puzzles, using their wits, and developing logical thinking, which will be useful for children in further exploring the world.
